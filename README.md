#  Pizza Sales Analysis — Python Data Analytics Project  
A complete end-to-end data analysis project using Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn & Plotly.

---

##  Overview

This project performs a **comprehensive analysis of pizza sales data** using Python.  
The goal is to extract insights, trends, and performance metrics that support business decisions related to:

- Revenue optimization  
- Sales forecasting  
- Menu engineering  
- Operational efficiency  
- Customer behavior insights  

The analysis includes KPI calculations, demand trends, product performance, and visual storytelling through charts.

---

##  Dataset

**File:** `pizza_sales.csv`

### **Key Columns**
- order_id – Unique order identifier  
- pizza_id – Pizza identifier  
- pizza_name – Name of the pizza sold  
- quantity – Number of pizzas per order  
- total_price – Total price of the pizza(s)  
- order_date – Date of purchase  
- order_time – Time of purchase  
- pizza_category – Category (Classic, Vegetarian, Supreme, etc.)  
- pizza_size – Size (S, M, L, XL)  
- pizza_ingredients – Ingredients list  

---

##  Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The script calculates:

- **Total Revenue**  
- **Total Pizzas Sold**  
- **Total Orders**  
- **Average Order Value (AOV)**  
- **Average Pizzas Per Order**

These metrics give a high-level overview of overall performance.

---

##  Analysis & Visualizations

###  1. Ingredient Analysis
- Identifies most frequently used ingredients.  
- Helps in **inventory planning** & **menu optimization**.

---

###  2. Daily Trends
Visualizations include:
- Total orders by day  
- Total revenue by day  
- Total pizzas sold by day  

**Purpose:** Weekly performance and staffing decisions.

---

###  3. Hourly Trends
Charts include:
- Total revenue by hour  
- Total orders by hour  
- Total sales by hour  

**Purpose:** Peak hours for kitchen load and customer rush.

---

###  4. Monthly Trends
- Monthly orders trend  
- Seasonality analysis  
- Demand forecasting  

---

###  5. Sales Distribution
- % of sales by pizza **category**  
- % of sales by **pizza size**  
- Heatmap: **Category × Size** revenue contribution  

---

###  6. Product-Level Performance

#### **Top 5 Best-Selling Pizzas**
Measured by:
- Quantity sold  
- Number of orders  
- Total revenue  

#### **Bottom 5 Worst-Selling Pizzas**
Measured by:
- Quantity  
- Revenue  
- Orders  

These help identify:
- High performers to promote  
- Low performers to modify or remove
